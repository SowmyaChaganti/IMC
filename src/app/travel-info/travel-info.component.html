Travel Information

If you decide to travel after you have received your card, you must carry:

  • A valid passport
  • Visa
  • I-20 (endorsed by your ISA within the last 6 months)
  • EAD
  • An employement verification letter from your employer

Note: Please note that these items do not guarantee re-entry.
